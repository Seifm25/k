Heritage and heart.

Fifty years in the making, this limited-edition sedan embodies everything that makes the 3 Series iconic: performance, efficiency, and striking style. With exclusive design elements, unique interior and exterior color combinations, and Extended Merino Leather upholstery included as standard for the first time in the U.S., this edition offers the opportunity to own a powerful piece of BMW history.

Curated color packages.

Five bold color schemes. Five iconic generations. Choose from five available BMW Individual paint finishes representing an iconic generation of 3 Series—Ruby Red Metallic, Boston Green Metallic, Avus Blue Metallic, Carbon Black Metallic, and Sparkling Graphite Metallic—each styled with complementary upholstery and brake calipers.

Mark the milestone.

Limited-edition badging on the door sill, cup holder cover, and rear trunk makes it clear that this performance sedan is a true rarity. The M Performance Exhaust and Carbon Fiber Rear Spoiler further underline its sporty pedigree.

Indulge yourself on every drive.

This is performance luxury at its finest. BMW Individual Extended Merino Leather upholstery comes standard exclusively on this model in three exquisite colors: Black, Ivory, and Tartufo. Striking Carbon Fiber trim completes the cockpit, making the interior of this special edition sedan feel as legendary as it looks.

Technical Specifications

The BMW 340i is more than just a car; it's a statement of engineering excellence. By stripping away 240 pounds of weight and retuning the chassis, BMW has created a beast that tames the track and the street alike.

Feature Specifications
Engine B58 3.0L Inline-6 (Turbo)
Horsepower 374 - 382 hp
Torque 500 Nm
Transmission 8-speed ZF Automatic
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) Approximately 4.4 seconds
Drivetrain RWD or xDrive (AWD)
Suspension M Sport (Adaptive optional)
Infotainment System BMW Curved Display with iDrive 8 / 8.5
Performance Features M Sport Brakes, M Differential, Sport Exhaust
Usage High performance & Daily practicality
